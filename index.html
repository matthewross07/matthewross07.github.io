Building cities, farming crops, and mining ore all have changed

the face of the earth. In many of these altered places,

human design decisions control environmental processes

more than the co-evolution of landform and ecosystem.

I study these human landscapes by asking:

1) How do these designed ecosystems work?

2) What feedbacks are most important in structuring these places?

3) Can we use knowledge from 1&2 to design ecosystems better?
